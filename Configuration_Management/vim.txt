Ctrl+E and Ctrl+Y shortcuts for Vim that respectively move the screenCtrl+E and Ctrl+Y shortcuts for Vim that respectively move the screen up and down with a one line step, without moving the cursor.

z, z - move current line to the middle of the screen(Note: Z, Z will save and close vim.)
z, t - move current line to the top of the screen
z, z - move current line to the bottom of the screen
